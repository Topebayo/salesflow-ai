About Us

Empowering African Businesses to Sell 24/7 on WhatsApp

SalesFlow AI was born out of a simple observation: WhatsApp is the lifeline of commerce in Africa, yet business owners spend hours responding to repetitive questions, qualifying leads, and manually closing sales.

We build state-of-the-art conversational AI agents tailored for Nigerian merchants and real estate companies. Our bots don't just chat—they qualify, showcase listings/catalogs, and guide leads directly toward inspections and purchases. Sales na water with SalesFlow AI!

Our Vision

Why SalesFlow AI?

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

At SalesFlow AI (a flagship SaaS product of NEXISFLOW LTD, RC 9659504), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the data of our merchants and their WhatsApp customers. This policy details how we handle information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the essential credentials required to run your WhatsApp AI integrations:

  • Account Details: Business name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Integration Data: WhatsApp Phone Number IDs, Meta API Access Tokens, and conversation histories needed to generate relevant AI sales replies.

2. How We Use Data

We use collected data solely to:

  • Generate accurate chat replies for your customer leads.
  • Display conversations, contacts, and lead qualification metrics in your merchant dashboard.
  • Improve AI prompt instruction accuracy for your specific business.

3. Data Security

All credentials and WhatsApp message streams are processed securely. Access keys are stored in encrypted environments, and communication is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS protocols.
