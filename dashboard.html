Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Phase 1: Meta Portal Setup
  1. Go to developers.facebook.com and create a Meta Business App.
  2. Add the WhatsApp product to your Meta App.
  3. Navigate to WhatsApp → API Setup to find your Access Token and Phone Number ID.
Phase 2: Save Credentials
  1. Paste credentials into the form above, then click Save Credentials.
  2. Copy the unique Webhook URL at the top of this page.
Phase 3: Webhook Verification
  1. On the Meta portal, go to WhatsApp → Configuration → Edit Webhook.
  2. Paste your Webhook URL and Verify Token, then click Verify and Save.
  3. Subscribe to the 'messages' webhook field.
